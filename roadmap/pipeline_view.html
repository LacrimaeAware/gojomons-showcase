Combat Systems Design

  • ✔️ Implement movesets (1)
    Notes
    Moveset system is functional. Future work focuses on improving depth and control.
    • ⬜ Implement cooldown system into moves (1)
      Notes

    • ⬜ Design best way to implement move biases for styles, and investigate pros/cons to cooldown system (2)
      Notes
      Define how combat styles (Tank, Sweeper, etc.) affect move logic, and compare cooldown vs. energy-based systems.
  • ⬜ Implement relics (Slay the Spire style) (1)
    Notes
    Relics influence combat and decision-making during runs. Fewer but more impactful than typical RPG relics.
    • ⬜ Concept: Gym leaders drop relic or equivalent (1)
      Notes
      Defeating gym leaders or special bosses yields unique relics that alter gameplay.
    • ⬜ Design relic ideas (3)
      Notes
      Brainstorm, balance, and finalize at least 20–30 relics that meaningfully affect combat.
  • ⬜ Implement 2v2 battle system (3)
    Notes
    Core battle format for mid/late game. Ideal target is 50/50 split between 1v1 and 2v2 encounters.
  • ⬜ Implement position-based and party-wide effects (2)
    Notes
    Add moves and abilities that affect positions or the entire team — e.g., cleaves hitting front/back, or auras buffing allies.
    • ⬜ Reflect on visual clarity for multi-target and position effects (1)
      Notes
      Consider UI/FX for indicating which units are hit or buffed. Possibly experiment with miniature party sprites or icon overlays.
  • ⬜ Fatigue and Timer System (1)
    Notes
    Future safeguard for endless battles or extreme scaling loops; triggers soft caps or bonuses after prolonged fights.

    Too much work and tonally off as a core system. Could survive as a side minigame where a Gojomon defends waves in a small arena (like a mini Vampire Survivors trial).
  • ⬜ Real-time trainer control (5)
    Notes
    Originally imagined as direct control of trainer in battle. Now replaced by auto-use item logic and potential single 'Mega Button' special form trigger.
  • ⬜ Metroidvania-style progression (3)
    Notes
    Lock certain routes or events behind abilities (Fly, Surf, etc.). Avoid full backtracking loops; focus on giving a sense of progression and world expansion.
  • ⬜ PvP implementation (∞)
    Notes
    Long-term goal after complete single-player balance. Could include asynchronous ghost battles or limited live matchmaking.
